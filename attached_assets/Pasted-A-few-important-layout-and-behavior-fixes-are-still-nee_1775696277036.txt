The opponent and date/time should stand out more at a glance.

- Either remove the emoji from Sports Tickets
OR

Everything should feel visually consistent and intentional across all buttons.

6. Overall mobile polish:
Please review these changes in mobile view specifically and make sure spacing feels balanced, premium, and easy to scan.
